Introduction Last updated: Oct 9, 2021

Welcome to CryptoShare! This is the technical documentation that explains the functionality of the Cryptoshare peer to peer ATM Crypto Lending Platform which will revolutionalize banking for underserved communities worldwide!

What is CryptoShare?

CryptoShare is a Peer-to-Peer Lending project that allows People to lend and borrow instantly. CryptoShare is being developed by the World Data Science Institute. CryptoShare will eliminate Credit Checks and give hardworking people access to lenders. This platform wants to provide Billions of people from around the world access to funding & capital. Over a billion people of color around the world do not even have access to a good enough credit rating to get a small loan. The platform provides a much needed alternative to the long daunting process of going through traditional credit approval to get funding. It could take years to build a good credit line. Borrowers don't want to wait 3 years to get funding to jumpstart a business. Oftentimes, the loans that people are approved for with a low credit rating means very high interest rates and high payments. We look to disrupt the continuous cycle that keeps people in debt and owing lenders. Borrowers will have innovative nontraditional ways to provide collateral.

The Importance of CryptoShare:

In the USA, African Americans have the lowest Credit scores and are declined for loans 3 times(Hispanic 2.5 times) as much as white applicants

In Latin America over 70% of the population do not have a bank account or good enough credit to get a loan from a bank!

500 million people in North & South America do not have acess to adequate loan products

African Americans and Hispanics are disproportionately forced to use Pawnshop loans, Cash Advance loans, and high interest peer to peer loans that can be up to 500%

How does CryptoShare work?

CryptoShare uses a combination of complex algorithms to streamline the process for users to be able to access funds. The simplistic front end design allows users to easily naviagate the application, so that they can send and use funds.

How CryptoShare works for Borrowers:

Step 1: User will receive a digital wallet and virtual debit card sent directly to their email address

Step 2: The borrower will have two options on how they would like to acess the funds. The borrower can choose either physical or digital collateral. If the Borrower(User) chooses digital collateral(cryptocurrency) this will be held in an escrow. Funds will instantly be avaialable in the Digital Wallet. If the user chooses physical collateral(diamond ring, trophy, collectible,etc.) then they must place the physical assest within a blockchain smart locker. Once the asset is verified and approved the funds will be instantly dispersed in the the user's digital wallet.

Step 3: Money is immediately avaialable to withdraw at an ATM or for use online with a Digital debit card.

How CryptoShare works:

Getting Started

This section provides an overview of how to get started with Crypto$hare.

Authentication

The intial step of registration involves authentication with the Apto Payments API/backend. Users can create an account with just their verified email, giving them an overview of the application. At this point, users will have limited access to give them the opportunity to see if Crypto$hare serves their needs without providing too much personal information.
